Mastodon Follow List POC

Single-file proof of concept. It registers an OAuth app on your home instance, stores client_id, client_secret, and access token in localStorage, then resolves and follows remote accounts through your instance. Requested scopes: profile read:accounts read:search read:follows write:follows.

Security note: storing OAuth secrets/tokens in localStorage is acceptable for a toy POC, not for a serious public app. Anyone who can run script in this origin can read them.
